Frequently Asked Questions

What is "No Win, No Fee"? +
"No Win, No Fee" means you pay absolutely nothing if we don't successfully recover your assets. Our fee is only a percentage of what we recover for you. The initial assessment is 100% free with no obligation.
How long does asset recovery take? +
Recovery times vary based on asset type and complexity. Simple cases can be resolved in 30-45 days, while complex cases may take 3-6 months. We provide a clear timeline after the free assessment.
What types of assets can you recover? +
We recover all types of assets including: unclaimed bank accounts, dividends, bonds, stocks, property deposits, pension funds, inheritance, insurance claims, and financial settlements. If it's rightfully yours, we can help recover it.
How much does it cost? +
The assessment is completely free. Our fee is only charged as a percentage of successfully recovered assets - typically 15-25% depending on case complexity. You only pay if we recover your assets, making it risk-free for you.
Is my information confidential? +
Absolutely. We maintain strict confidentiality protocols. All information is encrypted and protected. We never share your details with third parties without your explicit consent. Your privacy is our top priority.
What if I'm not sure if I have a claim? +
That's why we offer a free assessment! Submit your details and our experts will evaluate your case at no cost. We'll tell you if you have a valid claim and the probability of success before you commit to anything.
